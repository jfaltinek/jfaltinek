What is Just Call Jack?

My name is Jackson Faltinek and I am a college student at Loyola University Chicago. I started Just Call Jack handyman and yardwork last summer, and after a very successful few months and some great experience and learning, I have decided to take it up a notch with more offical bookings, services and prices. So if you need some yardwork taken care of or some handyman work done, Just Call Jack!
